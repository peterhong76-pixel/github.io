关于摩托油耗

摩托油耗是一款适用于 iPhone 和 Mac 的燃油记录工具， 可以记录加油金额、油价、里程及燃油标号，自动计算单次里程、 平均油耗、总油耗、总里程和燃油费用，并通过趋势图展示车辆的油耗变化。

iPhone 与 Mac 数据同步

本 App 使用 Apple iCloud 和 CloudKit 在设备之间同步数据。 请确认所有设备登录了同一个 Apple 账户，并已在系统设置中开启 iCloud。

常见问题

1. 为什么第一条记录没有单次油耗？

第一条记录用于建立里程基准。由于缺少上一条里程数据， 因此不会计算第一条记录的单次里程和油耗，这是正常情况。

2. 为什么 iCloud 图标显示感叹号？

  • 检查设备是否已登录 Apple 账户。
  • 确认系统设置中的 iCloud 和 iCloud Drive 已开启。
  • 确认网络连接正常。
  • 关闭并重新打开 App，然后点击同步按钮重试。
  • 新设备首次同步可能需要等待几分钟。

3. 为什么不同设备的数据没有立即出现？

iCloud 同步速度取决于网络及 Apple 服务状态。 请保持 App 联网，并等待片刻后重新同步。

4. 如何导入数据？

在 App 的记录页面打开更多菜单，选择导入功能， 然后选择受支持的 Excel 或数据文件。 导入前建议保留原始文件作为备份。

5. 如何修改或删除记录？

点击记录即可进入编辑页面。您可以修改内容并保存， 也可以在相应页面删除不需要的记录。

6. 如何切换语言或外观？

打开 App 的设置页面，可以选择简体中文、English 或跟随系统， 并可以选择浅色、深色或跟随系统外观。

7. 删除 App 会丢失数据吗？

仅保存在设备本地的数据可能会在删除 App 后丢失。 已同步至 iCloud 的数据通常仍保存在您的私人 iCloud 数据库中。 建议在删除 App 前保留数据备份。

About Moto Fuel Tracker

Moto Fuel Tracker is a fuel-recording app for iPhone and Mac. It records fuel cost, fuel price, odometer readings, and fuel grade, calculates trip distance and fuel consumption, and presents overall distance, fuel usage, cost, and consumption trends.

Sync Between iPhone and Mac

The App uses Apple iCloud and CloudKit to synchronize records between devices. Make sure each device is signed in to the same Apple Account and that iCloud is enabled in system settings.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Why does the first record have no trip fuel consumption?

The first record establishes the initial odometer baseline. Since there is no earlier record for comparison, trip distance and fuel consumption cannot be calculated for that entry.

2. Why does the iCloud icon display an exclamation mark?

  • Check that the device is signed in to an Apple Account.
  • Make sure iCloud and iCloud Drive are enabled.
  • Check your internet connection.
  • Restart the App and select the sync button again.
  • Initial synchronization on a new device may take several minutes.

3. Why do records not appear immediately on another device?

Synchronization time depends on the network connection and the availability of Apple’s iCloud services. Keep the App connected to the internet, wait briefly, and try syncing again.

4. How do I import data?

Open the additional options menu on the Records screen, select the import feature, and choose a supported Excel or data file. We recommend keeping the original file as a backup.

5. How do I edit or delete a record?

Select a record to open the editing screen. You can update and save its information or delete the record from the appropriate screen.

6. How do I change the language or appearance?

Open the App’s settings and select Simplified Chinese, English, or Follow System. You can also select Light, Dark, or System appearance.

7. Will deleting the App remove my data?

Data stored only on the device may be removed when the App is deleted. Records already synchronized to iCloud normally remain in your private iCloud database. We recommend keeping a backup before deleting the App.
