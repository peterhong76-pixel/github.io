常见问题Frequently asked questions

为什么第一条满箱记录没有油耗？Why does the first full tank have no consumption value?

它用于建立里程基准。第二次满箱后，App 才能使用里程差与区间内全部加油量计算油耗。

It establishes the odometer baseline. The next full tank provides the distance and interval fuel needed for a calculation.

部分加油如何计算？How are partial fills calculated?

部分加油不会独立产生油耗，会与之后的满箱记录合并成一个完整区间。油费仍会进入对应自然月的支出统计。

A partial fill does not create its own consumption result; it joins the next full-tank interval. Its cost still belongs to the calendar month in which it occurred.

为什么天然气和电力不在燃油标号中？Why are natural gas and electricity not fuel grades?

当前油耗模型使用升、元/升和 L/100km。天然气与电力需要 m³、kWh 等独立计量模型，直接混入会产生错误结果。

The current model uses liters, price per liter, and L/100km. Natural gas and electricity require separate m³ and kWh models; mixing them would produce incorrect results.

修改历史记录后会怎样？What happens after editing history?

日期、里程、金额、油价、加油量或满箱状态改变后，相关区间、趋势和汇总会自动重新计算。

Related intervals, trends, and summaries recalculate after changes to dates, odometer, cost, unit price, liters, or full-tank status.

另一台设备没有出现最新数据怎么办？What if another device does not show the latest data?

确认两台设备使用同一 Apple 账户并开启 iCloud Drive。先在来源设备点击同步，再在目标设备同步；若状态显示警告，请打开查看具体说明。

Confirm both devices use the same Apple Account with iCloud Drive enabled. Sync the source device, then the destination device, and open any warning status for details.

App 会追踪我吗？Does the app track me?

不会。当前版本没有广告、第三方分析或跨 App 追踪 SDK。详情请查看隐私政策。

No. The current version contains no ads, third-party analytics, or cross-app tracking SDKs. See the Privacy Policy for details.
