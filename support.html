常见问题Frequently asked questions

14 天试用会自动扣款或续费吗？Will the 14-day trial charge or renew automatically?

不会。试用需要你在 App 中主动确认，14 天结束后只会进入只读模式。只有你主动选择“永久解锁”并在 Apple 购买窗口确认，才会发生一次性购买。

No. You must actively confirm the trial in the app. After 14 days it simply becomes read-only. A one-time charge occurs only if you choose Lifetime Unlock and confirm Apple’s purchase sheet.

试用结束后数据会消失吗？Will my data disappear when the trial ends?

不会。已有数据仍可查看和导出，只是不能继续新增或编辑。永久解锁后会恢复完整编辑能力。

No. Existing data remains available to view and export; adding and editing are paused. Lifetime Unlock restores full editing.

以前已经付费购买过 App，还需要再买吗？I previously paid for the app. Must I buy it again?

不需要。符合条件的早期付费用户会自动识别为永久解锁。如状态没有正确恢复，请先确认使用原购买 Apple 账户，然后点按“恢复购买”。

No. Eligible original paid customers are recognized as permanently unlocked. If access is not restored, confirm you are using the Apple Account that made the original purchase, then choose Restore Purchases.

为什么第一条满箱记录没有油耗？Why does the first full tank have no consumption value?

第一条满箱记录用于建立里程基准。下一次满箱后，App 才能用里程差和区间内全部加油量计算油耗。

The first full tank establishes an odometer baseline. The next full tank provides the distance and interval fuel needed for a calculation.

自己的摩托照片为什么抠图不理想？Why is my motorcycle photo not isolated cleanly?

建议使用左右前方约 45°、两个车轮和后视镜完整、背景简单、没有强烈逆光的照片。编辑时把整辆摩托留在方框内，并将两个着地点放在轮胎与地面接触处。识别失败时可以改用普通裁剪或恢复默认街车。

Use a front three-quarter photo with both wheels and mirrors visible, a simple background, and no strong backlight. Keep the whole bike inside the editor and place both contact points where the tires meet the ground. If isolation fails, use standard cropping or restore the default street bike.

另一台设备没有最新数据怎么办？What if another device does not show the latest data?

确认两台设备使用同一 Apple 账户并开启 iCloud Drive。先在来源设备点按 iCloud 状态完成同步，再在目标设备同步。如果出现警告，打开状态查看针对该问题的解决选项。

Confirm both devices use the same Apple Account with iCloud Drive enabled. Sync the source device first, then the destination device. If a warning appears, open the status for issue-specific recovery choices.

App 会追踪我或上传票据照片吗？Does the app track me or upload receipt images?

不会为了广告追踪你。票据识别与摩托主体抠图在设备上完成，原始票据不会上传到开发者服务器。你选择保存的车辆照片可通过你的私人 iCloud 在设备之间同步。详情请查看隐私政策。

The app does not track you for advertising. Receipt recognition and motorcycle isolation run on-device, and source receipt images are not uploaded to a developer server. Motorcycle photos you save may sync through your private iCloud. See the Privacy Policy for details.
