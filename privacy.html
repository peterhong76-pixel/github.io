Moto Fuel Tracker saves data locally first and, when you use iCloud, syncs through your private CloudKit database. The current version contains no ads, third-party analytics, or cross-app tracking.

1. 本政策适用范围1. Scope

本政策适用于 Moto Fuel Tracker（中文名称“摩托油耗”）在 iPhone、iPad 和 Mac 上的数据处理。Apple 提供的 App Store、iCloud 与 CloudKit 服务另受 Apple 自己的条款和隐私政策约束。

This policy applies to data processing by Moto Fuel Tracker on iPhone, iPad, and Mac. Apple’s App Store, iCloud, and CloudKit services are also governed by Apple’s own terms and privacy policies.

2. 你创建的数据2. Data you create

根据你使用的功能，App 可能处理以下内容：

Depending on the features you use, the app may process:

  • 车辆名称、品牌、型号、年份、车牌、车辆照片及隐藏状态；Motorcycle name, make, model, year, plate, portrait, and hidden status;
  • 加油日期、金额、油价、加油量、累计里程、燃油标号、满箱状态和备注；Fuel date, cost, unit price, liters, odometer, grade, full-tank status, and notes;
  • 保养日期、里程、金额、地点、备注，以及保养、维修、零件、改装、用品、年检和保险等项目；Maintenance date, mileage, cost, location, notes, and items such as service, repairs, parts, modifications, accessories, inspection, and insurance;
  • 你主动选择导入或导出的 Excel 文件，以及本机自动备份。Excel files you choose to import or export, and local automatic backups.

3. 数据保存位置3. Where data is stored

App 会在设备本地保存记录，以便离线使用和自动恢复。当你登录 Apple 账户并使用 iCloud 时，车辆、照片、加油与保养数据会存入你自己的 CloudKit 私有数据库，用于你设备之间的同步。开发者没有提供用于集中存储这些记录的自有服务器。

The app stores records locally for offline use and automatic recovery. When you are signed in to an Apple Account and use iCloud, motorcycles, portraits, fuel, and maintenance data are stored in your private CloudKit database for sync between your devices. The developer does not operate a separate server that centrally stores these records.

4. 相机、照片和文字识别4. Camera, photos, and text recognition

只有在你主动使用车辆照片或票据识别功能时，App 才会请求相机或照片访问。票据、加油机或支付订单图片的文字识别在设备本机完成，用于建议日期、金额、油价、加油量、燃油标号或加油站文字；你可以在保存前修改或拒绝结果。用于识别的原始票据图片不会因该功能上传到开发者服务器。

The app requests camera or photo access only when you choose a motorcycle portrait or receipt-recognition feature. Text recognition for receipts, pumps, or payment images runs on-device to suggest dates, costs, unit prices, liters, fuel grades, or station text. You can edit or reject every suggestion before saving. Source receipt images are not uploaded to a developer-operated server for this feature.

5. Excel 导入与导出5. Excel import and export

App 只读取你通过系统文件选择器主动选择的文件。导出的工作簿由你决定保存或分享到何处。一旦你把文件交给第三方应用、云盘或其他人员，其后续处理受相应第三方的隐私政策约束。

The app reads only files you explicitly choose through the system file picker. You decide where an exported workbook is saved or shared. After you provide a file to another app, cloud service, or person, its handling is governed by that third party’s privacy policy.

6. 我们不做什么6. What we do not do

  • 不出售或出租你的个人信息或骑行数据；We do not sell or rent your personal information or riding data;
  • 不使用广告 SDK；We do not use advertising SDKs;
  • 不使用第三方行为分析 SDK；We do not use third-party behavioral analytics SDKs;
  • 不为了广告目的跨其他公司拥有的 App 或网站追踪你；We do not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies for advertising;
  • 不要求创建开发者账户。We do not require a developer-operated account.

7. 数据控制与删除7. Your controls and deletion

你可以在 App 中编辑或删除加油、保养和车辆记录，隐藏或恢复车辆，并通过数据管理导出或恢复数据。删除车辆会同时移除其加油、保养和照片数据；本机自动备份可能在有限时间内保留可恢复副本。你也可以通过 Apple 的系统设置管理 App 的照片、相机和 iCloud 权限。卸载 App 会删除其本地数据，但不会自动删除仍保存在你私人 iCloud 数据库或你自行导出的文件。

You can edit or delete fuel, maintenance, and motorcycle records; hide or restore motorcycles; and export or restore data through Data Management. Deleting a motorcycle also removes its fuel, maintenance, and portrait data, though a recoverable copy may remain briefly in local automatic backups. You can manage photo, camera, and iCloud permissions in Apple’s system settings. Removing the app deletes its local data but does not automatically delete data that remains in your private iCloud database or files you exported yourself.

8. 数据安全与保留8. Security and retention

App 依赖 Apple 平台提供的应用沙盒、系统权限和 CloudKit 安全机制。没有任何存储方式能够保证绝对安全。数据通常会保留到你在 App 中删除、清除相关 iCloud 数据、删除导出文件或移除本地备份为止。

The app relies on Apple platform protections including app sandboxing, system permissions, and CloudKit security. No storage method can guarantee absolute security. Data generally remains until you delete it in the app, remove the relevant iCloud data, delete exported files, or remove local backups.

9. 儿童隐私9. Children’s privacy

App 不以儿童为目标，也不会有意收集儿童的个人信息。如你认为儿童通过本 App 提供了个人信息，请联系开发者。

The app is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Contact the developer if you believe a child has provided personal information through the app.

10. 政策更新与联系10. Changes and contact

功能或数据处理方式发生实质变化时，本政策会更新生效日期。对本政策或数据处理有疑问，请通过 App Store 产品页面提供的开发者联系方式联系，并避免在支持信息中附上不必要的敏感数据。

If features or data practices change materially, this page will be updated with a new effective date. For questions about this policy or data handling, use the developer contact provided on the App Store product page and avoid including unnecessary sensitive data in support messages.

简要结论：In short: 你的数据主要保存在你的设备和你的私人 iCloud 中，开发者不靠追踪你的骑行来运营 App。Your data primarily stays on your devices and in your private iCloud; the app is not funded by tracking your riding activity.
