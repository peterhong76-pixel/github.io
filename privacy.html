摩托油耗隐私政策

生效日期：2026年7月4日

摩托油耗尊重并保护用户隐私。本应用不包含广告、用户追踪或第三方统计服务， 开发者不会出售或将用户数据用于广告营销。

1. 数据处理

用户输入的加油日期、消费金额、燃油价格、行驶里程、燃油标号等记录， 用于在设备上计算和展示油耗、里程及费用统计。

2. 本地存储

加油记录默认保存在用户设备本地。导入的表格数据也仅用于在应用内生成记录， 开发者不会接收导入的原始文件。

3. iCloud 同步

用户启用 iCloud 后，应用会使用 Apple CloudKit 将记录保存到用户的私人 iCloud 数据库，以便在用户自己的 Apple 设备之间同步。 开发者不运营独立的数据服务器，也不会查看或分析用户的私人加油记录。 Apple 对 iCloud 数据的处理适用 Apple 的隐私政策。

4. 数据共享

本应用不会向广告商、数据经纪商或其他第三方出售、出租或共享用户的加油记录。

5. 数据删除

用户可以在应用中修改或删除加油记录。删除操作会同步到已启用 iCloud 的其他设备。用户也可以在系统设置中关闭本应用的 iCloud 权限。

6. 儿童隐私

本应用不会主动收集儿童的姓名、联系方式、精确位置或其他身份信息。

7. 政策更新

如果本隐私政策发生重大变化，我们会更新本页面及生效日期。

Privacy Policy for Moto Fuel Tracker

Effective date: July 4, 2026

Moto Fuel Tracker respects your privacy. The app contains no advertising, user tracking, or third-party analytics. The developer does not sell user data or use it for advertising or marketing.

1. Data Processing

Fueling dates, expenses, fuel prices, odometer readings, and fuel grades entered by the user are used to calculate and display fuel consumption, distance, and cost statistics.

2. Local Storage

Fuel records are stored locally on the user's device. Imported spreadsheet data is processed only to create records inside the app. The developer does not receive the original imported files.

3. iCloud Sync

When iCloud is enabled, the app uses Apple CloudKit to store records in the user's private iCloud database and synchronize them across the user's Apple devices. The developer does not operate a separate data server and does not view or analyze users' private fuel records. Apple's processing of iCloud data is governed by Apple's privacy policy.

4. Data Sharing

The app does not sell, rent, or share fuel records with advertisers, data brokers, or other third parties.

5. Data Deletion

Users can edit or delete fuel records within the app. Deletions are synchronized across devices when iCloud is enabled. Users may also disable the app's iCloud access in system settings.

6. Children's Privacy

The app does not intentionally collect children's names, contact details, precise location, or other identifying information.

7. Changes

If this policy changes materially, this page and its effective date will be updated.
