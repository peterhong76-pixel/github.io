1. 概述

摩托油耗（以下简称“本 App”）尊重并保护您的隐私。本 App 用于记录加油信息、计算油耗与行驶里程，并通过图表展示油耗趋势。

2. 数据处理

本 App 允许您输入日期、加油金额、燃油价格、里程数、燃油标号等信息。 这些数据由您主动创建或导入，开发者不会通过独立服务器收集、查看或出售这些数据。

3. 本地存储

您的加油记录默认保存在设备本地。删除本 App 可能会删除仅保存在该设备上的数据，建议您定期备份重要记录。

4. iCloud 与 CloudKit

当您启用 iCloud 同步时，本 App 会使用 Apple CloudKit 在您登录同一 Apple 账户的 iPhone 和 Mac 之间同步数据。 相关数据存储于您的私人 iCloud 数据库中，开发者无法直接查看。 iCloud 数据的处理同时受 Apple 隐私政策及服务条款约束。

5. 数据导入

当您选择导入表格或数据文件时，本 App 仅在实现导入功能所需的范围内读取您主动选择的文件，不会扫描其他文件。

6. 数据收集与跟踪

  • 本 App 不包含第三方广告。
  • 本 App 不使用跨 App 或跨网站跟踪技术。
  • 本 App 不出售或出租个人信息。
  • 本 App 不使用第三方分析或营销 SDK。
  • 本 App 不要求注册开发者提供的独立账户。

7. 数据控制与删除

您可以在本 App 中添加、修改或删除加油记录。 您也可以通过系统的 iCloud 设置管理或删除相关云端数据。 卸载本 App 不一定会自动删除已经保存在 iCloud 中的数据。

8. 儿童隐私

本 App 不会有意收集儿童的姓名、联系方式、精确位置或其他身份识别信息。

9. 安全说明

我们通过 Apple 提供的系统存储与 CloudKit 服务保护数据。 但任何电子存储或网络传输方式均无法保证绝对安全。

10. 隐私政策变更

如果本政策发生重大变化，我们将更新本页面及生效日期。

11. 联系方式

如对本隐私政策有疑问，请联系： peterhong76@gmail.com

English

1. Overview

Moto Fuel Tracker (“the App”) respects and protects your privacy. The App helps you record fill-ups, calculate fuel consumption and distance traveled, and review fuel-consumption trends.

2. Data Processing

The App allows you to enter information such as the date, fuel cost, fuel price, odometer reading, and fuel grade. This information is created or imported by you. The developer does not collect, access, or sell this data through an independent server.

3. Local Storage

Your fuel records are stored locally on your device by default. Deleting the App may remove data stored only on that device. We recommend keeping backups of important records.

4. iCloud and CloudKit

When iCloud synchronization is enabled, the App uses Apple CloudKit to synchronize data between iPhone and Mac devices signed in to the same Apple Account. The data is stored in your private iCloud database and cannot be directly accessed by the developer. Apple’s privacy policy and service terms also apply to iCloud data.

5. File Import

When you import a spreadsheet or data file, the App reads only the file you explicitly select and only to the extent necessary to perform the import. It does not scan unrelated files.

6. Data Collection and Tracking

  • The App does not contain third-party advertising.
  • The App does not track users across apps or websites.
  • The App does not sell or rent personal information.
  • The App does not use third-party analytics or marketing SDKs.
  • The App does not require a separate developer-managed account.

7. Data Control and Deletion

You can add, edit, and delete fuel records within the App. You may also manage or delete related cloud data through your device’s iCloud settings. Uninstalling the App may not automatically delete data already stored in iCloud.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect children’s names, contact details, precise locations, or other identifying information.

9. Security

The App relies on Apple’s system storage and CloudKit services to protect data. However, no electronic storage or transmission method can guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes materially, this page and its effective date will be updated.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact: peterhong76@gmail.com
